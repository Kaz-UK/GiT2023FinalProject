About Us

Opened in 2009, our boardgame cafe has become the home to boardgames in Leeds. We offer a friendly and welcoming space for gamers young and old, with access to a large collection of boardgames.


Opening Times

We are open daily from 11am - 10pm


Game Sessions

We have three daily sessions that you can book.

Lunchtime: 11am - 1pm

Afternoon: 2pm - 5pm

Lunchtime: 6pm - 10pm


Game Library

We have a wide selection of games to choose from. Why not browse some of our titles online, or pop in to look through our game library.


Cafe

We are proud to support local producers and suppliers. Our cafe offers a range of freshly baked cakes and sandwiches between 11am-3pm, and from 5pm we offer snack food until 9pm.

We are fully licenced, and have a range of local craft beers on tap.


Booking FAQs

Can I stay for longer than the session I've booked

Your booking guarantees your table for the duration of your selected session. If on the day you would like to extend your session, please let our friendly staff know and we can try to accommodate your request if we have available space.

I want to book a table, but I don't know what game to play

We have a wide selection of games, and our staff are always happy to make suggestions.

I've booked a session, but I can no longer come

Please contact us on insert phone number to cancel your session.

I haven't booked, can I come along to a session?

We always recommend booking, but we welcome walk-ins where availability allows.

Privacy Policy

We are responsible with your data and never share it with anyone. Any data collected by us is stored on a password protected and secure device with single user access.
